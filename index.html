ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Sam! I’m a full stack web developer with a passion for creating meaningful user experiences on the web. I'm a proven problem solver with a passion for taking on new challenges. With my military and manufacturing background, I understand the importance of delivering results on time, on budget, and as promised.

In 2011, I graduated with a degree in Information Technology. Immediately following graduation, I commissioned into the Army as an officer in the Armor Branch. I spent the next 9.5 years building and leading teams to accomplish whatever mission we were handed. By the time I exited the service in August 2020, I had risen to the rank of Captain. After transitioning from the Army, I took a job as the Maintenance Coordinator for a Johnsonville plant (think sausage) in Momence, IL. After realizing that the manufacturing world is not for me, I decided to pivot my career back to my original interests – the tech world. While the Army provided me many opportunities, my IT skills degraded. I enrolled in the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp through the University of Washington to relearn my skills and to get up to date with industry best practices.
